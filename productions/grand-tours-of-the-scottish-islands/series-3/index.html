Grand Tours of the Scottish Islands - Series 3

In the second series of "Grand Tours of the Scottish Islands" Paul will once again embark on a travelogue around the myriad of islands that lay off our coastline. Beginning his odyssey on the sunny Isle of Coll, Paul will explore the islands of the west coast before heading north to Orkney.

To be broadcast on BBC1 Scotland in Autumn 2014.
