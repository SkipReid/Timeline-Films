Facelifts and Fillers

Our cameras follow the doctor and patients at one of Scotland’s busiest cosmetic medicine clinics...

About the show

This film gives a remarkable insight into what is now a huge part of everyday life in modern Scotland – the mission to stay forever young. With exclusive access to one of the country’s busiest clinics we will seek to understand just why Scots are flocking to get botox, fillers and facelifts, and meet the everyday people fuelling this multi-million pound industry. With nearly half of us saying that we are unhappy with how we look perhaps the person sitting next to you has had these treatments? Or maybe you’ve had it done but think you can get away without admitting it?

First broadcast on BBC1 Scotland Wed 17th Feb 2016 and repeated on BBC1 29th March 2016.
