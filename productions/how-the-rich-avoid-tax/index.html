How the rich avoid tax - C4 Dispatches

Actor Greg Wise uses undercover filming to investigate tax avoidance....

About the show

Inviting financial advisors into his home to find out how they advise rich clients to minimise their tax bills, Greg discovers that if you are wealthy enough, it’s possible to pay almost nothing to the tax man. As one advisor explains;

how much you want to pay depends on where you set your moral barometer

First broadcast on Channel 4: 8pm, Monday 8th Feb 2016.
