Timeline Films is a Scottish based independent television production company, making quality factual and documentary television programmes.

Timeline produces diverse factual and documentary television programmes that surprises and delights viewers with strong storytelling and high production values. Recent productions range from our new architecture series, Dreaming the Impossible: UnBuilt Britain for BBC4, Victorian Villains for BBC2 and the Mayan Apocalypse for C4.

The company’s Grand Tours of Scotland brand, presented by film-maker and historian Paul Murton, is about to enter its seventh series and has proved to be a major success for both BBC Scotland and BBC Network.
