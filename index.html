Timeline Films is a Scottish based independent media company producing quality factual television.

Timeline produces diverse content that surprises and delights viewers with strong storytelling and high production values. Recent productions range from our new architecture series, Dreaming the Impossible: UnBuilt Britain for BBC4, Victorian Villains for BBC Learning and the Mayan Apocalypse for C4.

The company’s Grand Tours of Scotland brand, presented by film-maker and historian Paul Murton, is about to enter its sixth series and has proved to be a major success for both BBC Scotland and BBC Network.
